# Q3 2024 Earnings Report

## Executive Summary

Meridian Technologies delivered strong results in Q3 2024, with total revenue of $42.3 million, representing 28% year-over-year growth. This exceeded our guidance of $39-41 million by a significant margin. The growth was driven primarily by enterprise customer expansion and new logo acquisition in the financial services vertical.

## Revenue Breakdown

### By Segment

- **Enterprise**: $28.7 million (68% of total), up 34% YoY
- **Mid-Market**: $9.8 million (23% of total), up 19% YoY
- **SMB/Self-Serve**: $3.8 million (9% of total), up 12% YoY

### By Geography

- **North America**: $31.2 million (74%), up 26% YoY
- **Europe**: $8.4 million (20%), up 35% YoY
- **Asia-Pacific**: $2.7 million (6%), up 41% YoY

## Key Metrics

- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: $171.6 million, up from $134.2 million in Q3 2023
- **Net Revenue Retention**: 118%, up from 112% in Q3 2023
- **Gross Margin**: 74.2%, compared to 72.8% in Q2 2024
- **Operating Margin**: -8.3%, improved from -12.1% in Q2 2024
- **Customer Count**: 1,247 paying customers (up from 1,089 in Q3 2023)
- **Enterprise Customers (>$100K ARR)**: 87, up from 64 in Q3 2023
- **Average Contract Value (Enterprise)**: $329,900

## Operating Expenses

- **R&D**: $14.8 million (35% of revenue), reflecting continued investment in the platform
- **Sales & Marketing**: $12.6 million (30% of revenue), including the new European sales team
- **General & Administrative**: $5.2 million (12% of revenue)
- **Total Operating Expenses**: $32.6 million

## Cash Position

- **Cash and equivalents**: $89.4 million
- **Operating cash flow**: -$2.1 million (improved from -$4.7 million in Q2)
- **Free cash flow**: -$4.3 million
- **Runway**: Approximately 24 months at current burn rate

## Notable Wins

- Signed GlobalTech Industries ($890,000 ARR), our largest deal to date
- Expanded Acme Corp contract from $240,000 to $520,000 ARR
- Launched in 3 new European markets (Germany, France, Netherlands)

## Guidance

For Q4 2024, we are guiding revenue of $44-46 million, representing 25-30% year-over-year growth. We expect to reach operating cash flow breakeven by Q2 2025.

## Outlook

The September search service outage impacted our enterprise pipeline. The Pinnacle Solutions deal ($340,000 ARR) was delayed by two weeks and we expect final signature in early Q4. Despite this setback, our enterprise pipeline remains healthy at $12.8 million in qualified opportunities.
