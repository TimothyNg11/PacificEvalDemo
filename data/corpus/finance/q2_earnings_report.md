# Q2 2024 Earnings Report

## Executive Summary

Meridian Technologies reported solid Q2 2024 results with total revenue of $38.1 million, representing 24% year-over-year growth. This was in line with our guidance of $37-39 million. Growth was primarily driven by expansion within existing enterprise accounts and strong adoption of our new Analytics Dashboard feature.

## Revenue Breakdown

### By Segment

- **Enterprise**: $24.9 million (65% of total), up 28% YoY
- **Mid-Market**: $9.1 million (24% of total), up 18% YoY
- **SMB/Self-Serve**: $4.1 million (11% of total), up 15% YoY

### By Geography

- **North America**: $29.0 million (76%), up 22% YoY
- **Europe**: $6.9 million (18%), up 31% YoY
- **Asia-Pacific**: $2.2 million (6%), up 38% YoY

## Key Metrics

- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: $155.8 million, up from $126.3 million in Q2 2023
- **Net Revenue Retention**: 115%, consistent with Q1 2024
- **Gross Margin**: 72.8%, compared to 73.1% in Q1 2024
- **Operating Margin**: -12.1%, improved from -14.6% in Q1 2024
- **Customer Count**: 1,198 paying customers (up from 1,042 in Q2 2023)
- **Enterprise Customers (>$100K ARR)**: 73, up from 58 in Q2 2023
- **Average Contract Value (Enterprise)**: $341,100

## Operating Expenses

- **R&D**: $13.9 million (37% of revenue), reflecting investment in the Analytics Dashboard
- **Sales & Marketing**: $11.4 million (30% of revenue)
- **General & Administrative**: $4.9 million (13% of revenue)
- **Total Operating Expenses**: $30.2 million

## Cash Position

- **Cash and equivalents**: $93.7 million
- **Operating cash flow**: -$4.7 million
- **Free cash flow**: -$6.8 million
- **Runway**: Approximately 28 months at current burn rate

## Notable Wins

- Signed Vertex Financial ($650,000 ARR), our first major financial services customer
- Renewed and expanded DataStream Corp from $180,000 to $310,000 ARR
- Analytics Dashboard reached 40% adoption among enterprise customers within 6 weeks of launch

## Guidance

For Q3 2024, we are guiding revenue of $39-41 million, representing 25-30% year-over-year growth. We continue to target operating cash flow breakeven by mid-2025.

## Outlook

Our enterprise pipeline is strong with $10.2 million in qualified opportunities. We expect the Q3 launch of the Advanced Search feature to drive additional upsell opportunities within our existing customer base. The planned expansion into three European markets will require upfront investment but is expected to contribute meaningfully to revenue by Q1 2025.
