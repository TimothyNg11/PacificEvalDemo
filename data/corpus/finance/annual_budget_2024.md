# Annual Budget 2024

## Budget Overview

The 2024 fiscal year budget for Meridian Technologies totals $128.5 million in planned expenditures against projected revenue of $158 million. This represents a planned operating loss of approximately $15 million as we continue to invest in growth while progressing toward profitability.

## Department Allocations

### Engineering — $52.3 million (40.7% of total budget)

- **Headcount**: 142 engineers (current) → 178 engineers (year-end target)
- **Compensation & Benefits**: $38.6 million
- **Cloud Infrastructure (AWS)**: $8.2 million
- **Tools & Licenses**: $3.1 million
- **Conferences & Training**: $0.8 million
- **Hardware for New Hires**: $1.6 million

Key investments:
- Expansion of the Document Processing Pipeline capacity (Q2)
- SOC 2 Type II certification completion (Q3)
- EU region infrastructure buildout (Q3-Q4)
- Advanced Search feature development (Q2-Q3)

### Sales & Marketing — $41.2 million (32.1% of total budget)

- **Headcount**: 68 (current) → 89 (year-end target)
- **Compensation & Benefits (incl. commissions)**: $28.4 million
- **Marketing Programs**: $7.8 million
- **Events & Conferences**: $2.4 million
- **Sales Tools (Salesforce, Gong, etc.)**: $1.8 million
- **Travel**: $0.8 million

Key investments:
- European sales team buildout (3 AEs, 2 SDRs, 1 Sales Manager)
- Enterprise marketing campaign targeting financial services vertical
- Annual user conference (MeridianConnect, October 2024)

### Product — $12.8 million (10.0% of total budget)

- **Headcount**: 18 (current) → 24 (year-end target)
- **Compensation & Benefits**: $9.2 million
- **User Research Tools & Programs**: $1.4 million
- **Design Tools & Licenses**: $0.6 million
- **Customer Advisory Board**: $0.4 million
- **Competitive Intelligence**: $1.2 million

### Human Resources — $8.4 million (6.5% of total budget)

- **Headcount**: 12 (current) → 15 (year-end target)
- **Compensation & Benefits**: $4.8 million
- **Recruiting (agencies, job boards)**: $2.1 million
- **Employee Programs (wellness, events)**: $0.9 million
- **HR Technology (Workday, Greenhouse)**: $0.6 million

### General & Administrative — $13.8 million (10.7% of total budget)

- **Executive Compensation**: $4.2 million
- **Legal**: $3.1 million
- **Office Leases**: $2.8 million (SF HQ + London office)
- **Insurance**: $1.4 million
- **Accounting & Audit**: $1.2 million
- **Other**: $1.1 million
