# Investor Update — October 2024

## Dear Investors,

We are pleased to share our October 2024 update following a strong Q3 that exceeded expectations across key metrics. Meridian Technologies continues to execute well against our strategic priorities.

## Business Highlights

### Revenue Momentum

Q3 revenue of $42.3 million represents our strongest quarter to date and a 28% year-over-year growth rate. We are raising our full-year 2024 revenue forecast to $162-165 million, up from our previous guidance of $155-160 million. We remain on track to reach $200 million ARR by the end of 2025.

### Enterprise Traction

Our enterprise segment continues to outperform, growing 34% year-over-year. We signed 23 new enterprise customers in Q3, bringing our total to 87. Our largest deal to date — GlobalTech Industries at $890,000 ARR — validates our push upmarket. The average enterprise contract value has grown to $329,900, up from $285,000 a year ago.

### European Expansion

Our European operations, launched in mid-2024, are performing ahead of plan. European revenue grew 35% year-over-year in Q3, driven by strong demand in the UK and initial traction in Germany and France. We expect Europe to represent 25% of total revenue by the end of 2025, up from 20% today.

## Strategic Priorities for H2 2024 and 2025

### 1. Path to Profitability

We have improved operating margin from -14.6% in Q1 to -8.3% in Q3 2024. We are targeting operating cash flow breakeven by Q2 2025 and GAAP profitability by Q4 2025. This will be achieved through revenue growth leverage and disciplined hiring — we will slow headcount growth from 27% in 2024 to approximately 15% in 2025.

### 2. Platform Expansion

We are investing in three new product capabilities:
- **Advanced Analytics**: Real-time usage analytics for enterprise customers (launching Q4 2024)
- **AI-Powered Summaries**: Automatic document summarization using LLMs (launching Q1 2025)
- **API v3**: A new API version with improved developer experience and webhooks (launching Q1 2025)

### 3. Strategic Partnerships

We have signed technology partnerships with Salesforce and ServiceNow for integration into their platforms. These partnerships are expected to drive $8-10 million in incremental ARR over 18 months through co-selling motions.

### 4. Market Positioning

Our competitive position has strengthened. According to the latest industry analyst reports, we are positioned as a "Strong Performer" in the document intelligence category, up from "Contender" a year ago. Our primary competitor, DocuVerse, has experienced leadership changes and slowed product development, creating an opportunity to capture market share.

## Key Risks

- **Customer Concentration**: Our top 10 customers represent 22% of ARR. We are actively diversifying.
- **Macroeconomic Environment**: Enterprise sales cycles have lengthened by approximately 2 weeks on average.
- **Technical Debt**: The September search outage highlighted the need for continued investment in reliability. Engineering is implementing comprehensive remediation.

## Upcoming Milestones

- Q4 2024: Advanced Analytics launch, MeridianConnect user conference
- Q1 2025: AI-Powered Summaries launch, API v3 launch
- Q2 2025: Operating cash flow breakeven target
- Q4 2025: GAAP profitability target

We remain confident in our trajectory and grateful for your continued support.
