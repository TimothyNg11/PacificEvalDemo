# Remote Work Policy

## Policy Overview

Meridian Technologies operates as a hybrid-first organization. We believe that flexibility drives productivity and well-being, while in-person collaboration drives innovation and team cohesion. This policy establishes guidelines for remote and in-office work.

## Work Arrangements

### Hybrid (Default)

The default work arrangement for all employees within commuting distance of an office:
- **In-office requirement**: Minimum 2 days per week (Tuesday and Thursday are designated "in-office" days)
- **Flexible days**: Monday, Wednesday, Friday are flexible — work from office or home
- **Core hours**: 10 AM - 3 PM local time for meetings and synchronous collaboration

### Fully Remote

Available for roles that have been designated as "remote-eligible" or for employees located more than 60 miles from a Meridian office:
- Must maintain regular working hours within their timezone
- Required to visit the nearest office quarterly for team events and planning sessions
- Travel expenses for quarterly visits are covered by the company (up to $2,500 per trip)
- Must have a dedicated workspace with reliable internet (minimum 50 Mbps)

### Office-Based

Required for certain roles that depend on physical presence:
- IT infrastructure team
- Office management
- Facilities

## Timezone Policy

Meridian operates across three primary timezones: Pacific (US HQ), Eastern (US East Coast team), and GMT/CET (European team).

Guidelines:
- All-company meetings are scheduled between 9 AM - 11 AM PT / 12 PM - 2 PM ET / 5 PM - 7 PM GMT
- Team-specific meetings should be scheduled during overlapping business hours
- Asynchronous communication is the default for cross-timezone collaboration
- Slack messages sent outside business hours should not expect a response until the next business day
- No meetings before 9 AM or after 6 PM in the employee's local timezone without explicit agreement

## Home Office Requirements

To ensure productivity and security, remote employees must maintain:
- A dedicated workspace (not a shared family area)
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps download)
- Company-provided laptop used for all work activities
- VPN connected when accessing internal systems
- Screen lock enabled with a maximum 5-minute timeout

## Communication Expectations

- **Slack**: Primary communication tool. Respond to direct messages within 4 hours during business hours.
- **Email**: Used for external communication and formal announcements. Respond within 24 hours.
- **Video Calls**: Camera on is encouraged but not required. Use virtual backgrounds if preferred.
- **Documentation**: All decisions must be documented in Confluence, not just discussed in Slack threads.

## International Remote Work

Employees may work from a country other than their employment country for up to 30 consecutive days per year, subject to:
- Manager approval (minimum 2 weeks notice)
- A time zone overlap of at least 4 hours with their team
- Compliance with local tax regulations (consult the Finance team)
- VPN usage for all work activities

Stays exceeding 30 days require People team and Legal approval due to tax and employment law implications.

## Expense Policy for Remote Workers

- **Home office setup**: One-time $1,500 stipend for desk, chair, monitor, etc.
- **Monthly internet**: $75/month reimbursement
- **Monthly wellness**: $75/month wellness stipend (same as in-office employees)
- **Co-working spaces**: Up to $300/month for co-working membership (with manager approval)

## Performance Expectations

Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as in-office employees. Output-based measurement is prioritized over hours worked. Remote work is a privilege that may be revoked if performance issues arise and are not resolved through the standard performance improvement process.
