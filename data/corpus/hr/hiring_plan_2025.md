# Hiring Plan 2025

## Overview

This document outlines Meridian Technologies' hiring plan for fiscal year 2025. We plan to grow from 318 employees (end of 2024 projected) to 385 employees by end of 2025, a 21% increase. This is a more measured growth rate compared to the 27% headcount growth in 2024, reflecting our path toward profitability.

## Hiring by Department

### Engineering — 32 New Hires (Target: 210 total)

**Current Headcount**: 178 (projected EOY 2024)

Priority roles:
- **Backend Engineers (12)**: Support growing product surface area and platform scalability
  - 4 for the new AI Features team (document summaries, smart search)
  - 4 for the Platform team (API v3, multi-region)
  - 4 for reliability and infrastructure improvements
- **Frontend Engineers (6)**: Build out the Advanced Analytics Dashboard and redesigned admin console
- **ML Engineers (4)**: Expand the ML team for AI-powered features (currently 3 ML engineers; target: 7)
- **Site Reliability Engineers (3)**: Support the growing infrastructure footprint across US and EU regions
- **QA Engineers (4)**: Improve automated testing coverage, particularly for enterprise integrations
- **Engineering Managers (3)**: Support the growing team (target: 1 manager per 8-10 engineers)

Timeline:
- Q1: 10 hires (focus on ML and backend engineers)
- Q2: 10 hires (frontend and SRE)
- Q3: 8 hires (remaining backend and QA)
- Q4: 4 hires (backfill and emerging needs)

## Recruiting Strategy

### Sourcing Channels

Based on 2024 hiring data, our most effective sourcing channels are:
1. **Employee referrals**: 34% of hires, highest conversion rate (42% from referral to offer)
2. **LinkedIn Recruiter**: 28% of hires
3. **Inbound applications**: 18% of hires
4. **Recruiting agencies**: 12% of hires (used primarily for executive and specialized roles)
5. **University recruiting**: 8% of hires

### Recruiting Metrics Targets for 2025

| Metric | 2024 Actual | 2025 Target |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| Time to fill (days) | 42 | 38 |
| Offer acceptance rate | 78% | 82% |
| Cost per hire | $8,400 | $7,500 |
| Employee referral rate | 34% | 40% |
| Diversity hiring (URG) | 31% | 35% |
| 90-day retention | 96.4% | 97% |

### Employer Branding

Investment in employer branding for 2025:
- Launch engineering blog with monthly technical posts
- Sponsor 4 industry conferences (2 engineering, 1 product, 1 sales)
- University partnerships with 3 target schools (Stanford, CMU, Georgia Tech)
- "Life at Meridian" social media campaign

## Budget

Total recruiting budget for 2025: $2.8 million
- Agency fees: $950,000
- Job board subscriptions: $380,000
- Employer branding & events: $420,000
- Recruiting tools & technology: $250,000
- Relocation assistance: $500,000
- Employee referral bonuses: $300,000 ($5,000 per referral hire)

## Compensation Adjustments

To remain competitive in the 2025 market, we are planning:
- **Merit increases**: Average 4.5% pool (allocated based on performance rating)
- **Market adjustments**: Additional 2-3% for roles where we've fallen below the 65th percentile
- **Equity refresh**: Annual refresh grants totaling $4.2 million in equity value
