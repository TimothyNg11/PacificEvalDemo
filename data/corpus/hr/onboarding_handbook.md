# Onboarding Handbook

## Welcome to Meridian Technologies

Congratulations on joining the Meridian team! This handbook provides a comprehensive guide for your first 90 days. Our goal is to get you productive, connected, and comfortable as quickly as possible.

## Before Day 1

Your hiring manager and the People team will ensure the following are ready before your first day:

- Laptop (MacBook Pro 16" for engineering, MacBook Air for all other roles)
- Accounts provisioned: Google Workspace, Slack, GitHub, Jira, Confluence
- Badge access to the San Francisco headquarters (if applicable)
- Welcome kit shipped to your address (company swag, notebook, headphones)

## Week 1: Orientation

### Day 1
- Morning: Welcome session with the People team (company overview, values, policies)
- Afternoon: Setup your development environment (engineering) or core tools (non-engineering)
- End of day: 1:1 with your manager to discuss first-week goals

### Day 2-3
- Complete all required compliance training (Security Awareness, Anti-Harassment, Data Privacy)
- Shadow a team member on their daily work
- Tour of the product — hands-on walkthrough with a Product team member

### Day 4-5
- Meet with your onboarding buddy (assigned during pre-boarding)
- Start reviewing team documentation and current sprint backlog
- Attend your first team standup

## Week 2-4: Ramp-Up

### Engineering New Hires
- Complete the Engineering Bootcamp (5-day self-paced curriculum):
  1. Architecture Overview walkthrough
  2. Local development setup
  3. Deploy a test change to staging
  4. Write and submit your first PR
  5. On-call shadowing (1 day)
- Target: Ship your first production PR by end of Week 3

### Non-Engineering New Hires
- Complete role-specific training modules
- Job shadow with 3-4 team members across different functions
- Attend cross-functional meeting as an observer

## Days 30-60: Building Momentum

- Take ownership of at least one project or initiative
- Complete a "Customer Immersion" session — listen to 3 recorded customer calls
- Schedule skip-level 1:1 with your manager's manager
- Provide feedback on the onboarding process (anonymous survey)

## Days 60-90: Full Integration

- Complete your first performance check-in with your manager
- Set quarterly OKRs aligned with team goals
- Identify one area for process improvement and propose a solution
- Present your 90-day learnings to your team

## Key Resources

- **IT Help Desk**: #it-help on Slack, or helpdesk@meridian.tech
- **People Team**: #people-team on Slack, or people@meridian.tech
- **Onboarding Buddy Program**: Your buddy is your first point of contact for informal questions
- **Employee Assistance Program (EAP)**: Confidential counseling and support, details in Workday

## Onboarding Metrics

We track the following metrics to continuously improve our onboarding process:
- **Time to First PR** (engineering): Target 15 days, current average 12 days
- **Time to First Customer Interaction** (sales/CS): Target 10 days, current average 8 days
- **New Hire NPS** (30-day survey): Current score 72 (target: >60)
- **90-Day Retention Rate**: 96.4% (industry average: 89%)

## Company Values

1. **Customer Obsession** — Every decision starts with the customer
2. **Bias for Action** — Move fast, iterate, ship
3. **Radical Transparency** — Default to sharing information
4. **Continuous Learning** — We invest in growth, personally and professionally
5. **One Team** — No silos, no politics, we win together
