# Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

Meridian Technologies uses a value-based pricing model anchored on per-user-per-month licensing. Our pricing reflects the ROI our customers achieve through improved document search efficiency and knowledge worker productivity. Internal studies show that Meridian saves enterprise customers an average of 45 minutes per employee per week in document search time.

## Current Pricing Model

### Pricing Tiers

| Feature | Standard ($25/user/mo) | Professional ($45/user/mo) | Enterprise ($75/user/mo) |

| Support | Email (24h) | Priority (4h) | Dedicated (1h) |
| SSO/SAML | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Audit Logging | ✗ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Data Residency | US only | US only | US, EU, APAC |
| Uptime SLA | 99.9% | 99.95% | Custom (up to 99.99%) |
| CSM | ✗ | ✗ | Dedicated |
| Min. Users | 10 | 50 | 100 |

### Annual Billing

All pricing above is for annual billing (paid annually). Monthly billing is available at a 20% premium. Approximately 85% of customers are on annual billing.

## Discounting Framework

### Approved Discounts

| Discount Type | Approval Required | Maximum |
|--------------|-------------------|---------|
| Volume (100-499 users) | AE | 10% |
| Volume (500-999 users) | AE | 15% |
| Volume (1,000+ users) | AE | 20% |
| Multi-year (2 years) | Sales Manager | 10% |
| Multi-year (3 years) | VP Sales | 15% |
| Competitive displacement | VP Sales | 20% |
| Strategic/logo deal | CRO | 25% |

**Maximum stackable discount**: 30% (requires CRO approval). No deal should ever exceed 30% off list price.

### Discounting Guidelines

1. **Never lead with discounts** — always establish value first
2. **Time-bound concessions** — discounts expire in 30 days
3. **Quid pro quo** — every discount should come with a customer commitment (case study, reference call, multi-year, etc.)
4. **Floor price**: Professional tier floor is $32/user/month. Enterprise tier floor is $52/user/month.

## Competitive Pricing Intelligence

| Competitor | Pricing | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| DocuVerse | $15-40/user/month | Aggressive pricing, especially for SMB. Free tier available. |
| SearchPro | $20-55/user/month | Open-source core is free. Enterprise add-ons are premium. |
| LegacyCorpSearch | $50-80/user/month | Higher price, long-term contracts (2-3 year minimum). |

### Competitive Positioning

- **vs. DocuVerse**: We are ~60% more expensive at the Standard tier but offer significantly better search quality. Position as "invest in quality" — quantify the ROI difference.
- **vs. SearchPro**: Comparable pricing. Differentiate on enterprise features and professional services.
- **vs. LegacyCorpSearch**: We are cheaper with a better product. Easy win on pricing and technology.

## Price Increase Strategy

We plan to implement a 5-8% list price increase effective January 2025. Existing customers on annual contracts will be grandfathered at current rates until renewal. The increase reflects:
1. Enhanced feature set (hybrid search, reranking, audit logging added since last pricing update)
2. Market positioning (moving upmarket, should price accordingly)
3. Inflation and cost increases

Communications plan:
- Announce to existing customers via email in November 2024
- 60-day notice period before new pricing takes effect
- Offer existing customers the option to lock in current pricing with a 2-year renewal
