# Customer Case Studies

## Case Study 1: DataStream Corp

### Company Overview
DataStream Corp is a mid-size data analytics company with 340 employees across offices in Austin, TX and Chicago, IL. They manage over 85,000 internal documents including technical specifications, research reports, and client deliverables.

### Challenge
Before Meridian, DataStream relied on a shared Google Drive with folder-based organization. Engineers spent an average of 52 minutes per day searching for relevant documentation. New hires reported that the onboarding process was significantly hampered by the inability to find institutional knowledge.

"Our engineers were spending more time looking for documents than actually reading them. It was frustrating and incredibly wasteful." — Maria Santos, VP of Engineering, DataStream Corp

### Solution
DataStream deployed Meridian Professional Tier for their engineering and research teams (180 users). The implementation took 3 days, including document ingestion of 85,000 documents and SSO configuration with their Okta instance.

### Results
After 6 months of Meridian deployment:
- **Time saved**: Average document search time reduced from 52 minutes/day to 11 minutes/day — a 79% reduction
- **Onboarding**: New hire ramp-up time decreased from 8 weeks to 5 weeks
- **Adoption**: 94% weekly active usage rate among licensed users
- **ROI**: Estimated $1.2 million annual productivity savings based on time saved per employee
- **Satisfaction**: Internal NPS score for the tool: 67

"Meridian has fundamentally changed how our team accesses knowledge. The hybrid search feature is remarkably good at finding relevant documents even when you don't know the exact terms to search for." — Maria Santos, VP of Engineering, DataStream Corp

DataStream expanded their Meridian deployment from Professional ($180K ARR) to Enterprise ($310K ARR) in Q2 2024, adding their product management and client services teams.

## Case Study 2: Thornton Legal Group

### Company Overview
Thornton Legal Group is a 120-attorney law firm specializing in intellectual property and corporate law. They manage approximately 250,000 legal documents including case files, contracts, precedents, and regulatory filings.

### Challenge
Thornton was using LegacyCorpSearch for document management, paying $62 per user per month. The system required dedicated IT staff to maintain and searches regularly returned irrelevant results. The firm estimated that attorneys spent 8-10 hours per week on document research that could be more efficient.

"Our old system was like searching the internet in 1999. You had to know exactly what keywords to use, and even then you'd miss important precedents." — James Thornton, Managing Partner

### Solution
Thornton deployed Meridian Enterprise Tier for all attorneys and paralegals (150 users). Migration from LegacyCorpSearch took 2 weeks, including document migration and custom metadata mapping.

Key features utilized:
- Hybrid search with reranking for high-precision legal document retrieval
- Audit logging for compliance and client billing
- Data residency (US) for regulatory compliance

### Results
After 4 months:
- **Search precision**: Relevant document retrieval improved from 45% (LegacyCorpSearch) to 87% (Meridian)
- **Attorney time saved**: 6.2 hours per attorney per week on document research
- **Cost savings**: $18/user/month savings vs. LegacyCorpSearch, plus $800,000 annual productivity improvement
- **Client satisfaction**: 15% reduction in case preparation time, leading to improved client outcomes
- **Compliance**: Passed audit compliance review with zero findings related to document management

"The ROI was obvious within the first month. Our attorneys are spending time on legal strategy instead of searching through filing cabinets — physical and digital." — James Thornton, Managing Partner

## Case Study 3: NovaTech Manufacturing

### Company Overview
NovaTech is a manufacturing company with 2,100 employees producing precision components for the aerospace industry. They manage technical specifications, quality control documents, and regulatory compliance records totaling over 400,000 documents.

### Challenge
NovaTech was using DocuVerse for document search but found that search relevance was inadequate for their technical documents. Engineering specifications contain highly specialized terminology, and DocuVerse's vector-only search frequently missed documents that used alternative technical terms for the same concepts.

### Solution
NovaTech switched to Meridian Enterprise Tier for their engineering and quality teams (500 users). The key selling point was Meridian's hybrid search with reranking, which handles the terminology variations common in technical documentation.

### Results
After 3 months:
- **Search recall**: Improved from 64% (DocuVerse) to 89% (Meridian) on their internal benchmark
- **Quality incidents**: 23% reduction in quality incidents attributed to engineers finding relevant specifications more quickly
- **Compliance**: Automated compliance document retrieval for FAA audits, reducing audit preparation from 3 weeks to 4 days
- **Annual contract**: $380,000 ARR on a 2-year contract

"In aerospace, finding the right specification isn't just about efficiency — it's about safety. Meridian's search quality gives us confidence that we're not missing critical documents." — Robert Kim, Chief Quality Officer, NovaTech
